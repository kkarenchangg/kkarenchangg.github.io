Overview

Working with our team of designers, web project manager, and web developer at Raindrop Marketing, we created various options of the Souplantation/Sweet Tomatoes Restaurant homepage. This was one direction presented as a solution to the needs and preferences the brand had.

brand notes/ Goals

Through their website remodel, Souplantation/Sweet Tomatoes wanted to achieve a handful of goals:
- Stand out as a relevant industry leader with their new branding
- Highlight their workers' stories
- Modern site that is interactive & fun, yet simple and easy to navigate
- Intentional showcasing of seasonal items

Targeted Audiences

Souplantation/Sweet Tomatoes was looking to branch out towards the younger family dynamic with their new branding. Their current advertising and brand catered very well to older audiences, but they wanted to also incorporate a youthful approach to invite younger parents to bring their families to the restaurants.

Wireframes / iteration

The wireframes had to include various aspects that were interactive yet informative. Hover effects were added to incorporate interest and movement to the page without overwhelming the user. Paralellograms were used rather than regular blocks for each section as well to incorporate something that could be visually appealiing to younger ages.

There were two directions that the menu panels could be: exposed and fixed or hidden, and the exposed menu was ultimately chosen. The month's seasonal menu items have also been added in the dropdown of the "Menu" tab to allow for a call out on top of the section dedicated to these.

Lastly, another section was incorporated to introduce the new ad campaign. These ads contained different characters made from various flavor combinations, and incorporating an interactive aspect to the site using these characters would be able to help the new campaign branding be more recognizable to the user.
