Given just a script and some time, I created two fully animated explainer videos from storyboard to finishing touches. I was mentored and advised by my art director, and worked closely with the project manager and client to portray their vision in an accurate and beautiful manner.

THOUGHTS / WHAT I LEARNED

I never would have thought that I would be able to make my sketches come to life when I first started hand lettering. However, during my time at Raindrop, I was fascinated by creating GIFs and social media posts and was eager to learn more. These two videos expanded my knowledge of what animation can offer, and easily became some of the projects I am most proud of.
