Hand Lettering

Where it all began...

It all started with a couple doodles. I wanted to make a card for a friend, and I came across a hand lettering account on Instagram for some inspiration. A few months later, I pushed myself to start a 100 day challenge, and decided that I wanted to sharpen my lettering skills.

View All

Hand lettering instantly became a full-blown passion when a friend generously lent me an iPad Pro. Going digital with my lettering allowed me to share my work more easily and offer my skill set in even more ways.

Lettering became more than just a hobby at this point - it became a way for me to connect with others and help people transform ideas into reality. Even though I had no background in design, I had an urge to just learn as much as I could so I could partner with others and create something amazing together.

Lighthouse Bible Church

Created with ProCreate and Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign
Graphic Design, Apparel Design, Social, Handlettering, Illustration
‍
I have been creating pieces for Lighthouse Bible Church since around 2016, where I decided to branch out further from hand lettering into graphic design. Throughout the years, I have had the privilege of making wall prints, banners, apparel designs, programs, social media posts, and other print work.

At Lighthouse, I have been able to work with design students to graphic designers at various levels of expertise. I am grateful to have learned most of my initial steps of design practices and terms through this team, as well as contribute my hand lettering into my designs. I also collaborate with the web team to create assets as announcements and events as they come up.
