Nice to Meet You.

Thanks for taking a few minutes to see a window into my life. To be very honest, I had a bit of a unique journey to get where I am today. I graduated with a Psychology Major and Biology Minor at UC San Diego, initially set to become a Pediatric Occupational Therapist. It was purely my love for design, and the way I was able to help others form their creative dreams into realities, that launched me into a professional career in this field. Since then, I have been able to collaborate with a wonderfully talented group of creators at Raindrop Marketing in San Diego, where I was trained up to have a professional work process and a heart to not just create good work, but good relationships. Because my greatest motivation is learning what I do not know, I'm constantly seeking new techniques, styles, and platforms to design on. I found that studying Psychology has come in handy to understand the human mind, which is where the beauty of Design began in the first place. As for Occupational Therapy, I don't think I'll be turning back any time soon.

When I'm not designing, you can find me dabbling in various crafts such as stamp carving, painting, and photography. And when I'm not doing that, I will most likely be tending to my plant family, reading, or finding someone's puppy to befriend (like Buddy, pictured above).

Want to create something great together? Shoot me an email!
