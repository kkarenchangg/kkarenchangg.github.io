ABOUT ME



Hi hello! My name is Karen Chang.

​

I'm a recent graduate from University of California, San Diego where I studied Physiology & Neuroscience, Psychology, and Biology. Why am I here then, you ask? It's because my love and passion for visual design pushed me to take a leap of faith into the world of graphic design professionally. Up until this past year, I have been a self-taught hand-letter, graphic designer, and illustrator. Beginning early 2018, I enrolled in the Graphic Design Specialized Certificate Program at the UCSD Extension, and have been loving every moment of it. I am eager to see what is in store for me next in this journey! ​
