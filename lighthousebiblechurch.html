Overview

I have been creating pieces for Lighthouse Bible Church since around 2016, where I decided to branch out further from hand lettering into graphic design. Throughout the years, I have had the privilege of making wall prints, banners, apparel designs, programs, social media posts, and other print work.

At Lighthouse, I have been able to work with design students to graphic designers at various levels of expertise. I am grateful to have learned most of my initial steps of design practices and terms through this team, as well as contribute my hand lettering into my designs. I also collaborate with the web team to create assets as announcements and events as they come up.

Goals

I wanted to create eye-catching designs that were most importantly consistent throughout Lighthouse's branding. There are many people that work on different assets for the church, so often times even one program can have many different fonts and ideas on it. My goal was to create designs that not only provided all the information needed, but also were easy to follow and cohesive overall.

Retreat Booklet Design & Layout

I was given full freedom for the directions that each of these projects went in. Each retreat had a different theme, and slogan given by the speaker that year, and I was asked to create a design that went with the message that the pastor would provide, or something that would match the audience of that retreat. Over the years I have played with different hand lettered fonts, illustrations, and layouts to allow for the information to be easily displayed and quickly found.
